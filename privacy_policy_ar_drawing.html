Effective date: October 6, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how AR Drawing - Zero to Hero ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our Android application. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. What the App Does

The App uses augmented reality (AR) technology to help users trace and learn drawing. It allows users to project images through their phone camera onto real-world surfaces, adjust transparency and alignment, and trace outlines on paper. Users can also access tutorials, adjust drawing settings, and save or share their artwork.

⚠️ AR Safety Warning

Please use AR features responsibly and safely:

  • Parental Supervision: Children should use the AR mode under the supervision of a parent or guardian.
  • Be Aware of Your Surroundings: Always stay alert and ensure you are in a safe environment when using the AR camera. Avoid obstacles, traffic, and other hazards.

By using AR features, you acknowledge these safety reminders and agree to use the App in a safe and responsible manner.
