1. What the App Does

The App is a free video player and text-to-speech utility for Android phones and tablets. It allows users to play local media, download videos and audio from supported online sources, convert text files to spoken audio, manage files, and secure personal videos in a password-protected folder. Key features include offline viewing, popup player, background playback, multi-download support, and a read-aloud function for documents (PDF, DOCX, etc.).
